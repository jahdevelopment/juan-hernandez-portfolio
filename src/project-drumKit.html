Drum kit Game

With this program you will have 6 keys that represent different drums in a typical drum set when you clicked on one of these buttons what you will get is the corresponding sound on the drum. In addition, you can use the keys on the keyboard. You could try and play different rhythms.

This is an excercise project developed in the online course "The complete 2022 Web Development bootcamp - by Angela Yu" on the web platform https://www.udemy.com/
