  • Colombian meals for a day

    Would you like to learn how to cook and eat Colombian meals for an entire day? If yes, this is the right lecture, come on and follow my steps!

    First of all, it is important to clarify that Colombian food is rich in the next ingredients:

    • Carbohydrates
    • Gluten
    • Meat
    • Chicken
    • Pork
    • Milk

    Having in mind the last information, if you do not have problems with any of those, you could try making any of the next recipes and learn a little bit about our culture.

    One of the positive points of the pandemic is that more people took advantage of their spare time at home to learn something new, I did that with the kitchen and made the purpose to learn to cook different dishes for my family, and I enjoyed it.

    Now I will share with you one of my new hobbies, giving simple examples of three meals of a regular Colombian family, with ingredients easy to find in any supermarket and quantities for cooking for three people.

    • Start with breakfast to start a day with a recharge of energy.
    • Continue with lunch which is the main meal of the day.
    • Finalize the dinner and you are ready to go with a full stomach but a happy heart.

        Breakfast: Orange juice, hot chocolate with cloves and cinnamon, scrambled ranch eggs.

        Ingredients Quantity
        Orange 6 units
        Milk 1 litter
        Chocolate powder 3 tablespoons
        Cloves 1 piece
        Cinnamon 2 sticks
        Eggs 6 units
        Sausage 2 units
        Yellow corn 4 tablespoons
        Oil 1 tablespoon
        Salt 1 tablespoon

        Instructions:

        • Squeeze all the oranges and leave the entire sumo.
        • Heat the milk in a saucepan over medium heat and add the chocolate powder. When it is boiling, lower the temperature and add the clove and cinnamon sticks, leave it for heating for 5 more minutes.
        • Cut the sausages into slices.
        • Scramble the eggs, and add the salt.
        • Heat the saucepan with the oil of your preference, add the scrambled eggs, the sausage slices and the corn, mix all of them and let them cook.

        Everything is ready to serve, you can add a piece of cheese and bread to string along with the ranch eggs.

        Lunch: Rice with chicken.

        Ingredients Quantity
        Chicken 1 breast
        Rice 2 cups
        Water 4 cups
        Oil 3 talespoons
        Red pepper 1 large piece
        Carrot 1 piece
        Yellow corn 4 tablespoons
        Peas 4 tablespoons
        Tomato ketchup 2 tablespoons
        Garlic 1 clove
        Salt 4 tablespoons
        Sugar 1 tablespoon
        Onion 1 medium piece

        Instructions:

        • Cook the chicken breast with water and 1 tablespoon of salt.
        • Cut the chicken breast into small pieces.
        • Chop the carrot and the peas into small pieces.
        • Boil the veggies (carrot, peas and rice) for 15 minutes in a pot with water, one tablespoon of salt and one tablespoon of sugar.
        • Heat the oil in a large pot with salt, onion and garlic, over low heat. Once everything is turning golden, add the 2 cups of rice, the 2 tablespoons of ketchup and the 4 cups of water, stir all the ingredients and increase the temperature.
        • Cover the pot once the rice has absorbed the water, add the veggies and the chicken pieces, stir all again, and cook at low heat for 15 more minutes until the rice is turning soft.

        Usually, this dish is accompanied by fries, tomato sauce and lettuce on the border of the plate.

        Dinner: Stuffed potatoes with ground beef.

        Ingredients Quantity
        Potatoes 12 units
        Ground beef 250 Gr.
        Tomatoes 2 units
        Onion 80 Gr.
        Garlic 1 clove
        Black pepper 1 tablespoon
        Salt 1 tablespoon

        Instructions:

        • Wrap each of the potatoes in aluminum foil and cook in the oven at 200ºC for about 60 minutes or until they are soft.
        • Chop the tomato and the onion, and boil them in a saucepan with oil, salt, black pepper and garlic.
        • When the potatoes are ready, cut each one in the middle, and make a space in the interior with a spoon. Keep the potato pulp extracted from the spaces.
        • Add the ground beef to the tomato and onion cooked, stir all of them and cook until the beef is turning dark and a little golden.
        • Mix the ground beef with the potato pulp, make a mass and boil again.
        • Full the potatoes with the mass of potato and ground beef and serve it.

        Finally, feel free to include more ingredients like cheese, sausages, chicken, bacon or anything of your preference, or just decorate the potatoes with some aromatic herb or vinegar sauce that makes your plate a little bit fancy.
